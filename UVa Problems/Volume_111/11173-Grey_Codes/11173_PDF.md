# 11173 - Grey Codes

We are going to generate a sequence of integers in binary. Start with the sequence

$$
\begin{align}
0 \\
1 \\
—
\end{align}
$$

Reflect it in the horizontal line, prepend a zero to the numbers in the top half and a one to the numbers on the bottom and you will get

$$
\begin{align}
{\color{red}0}0 \\
{\color{red}0}1 \\
— \\
{\color{blue}1}1 \\
{\color{blue}1}0 \\
\end{align}
$$

Repeat this again, and you will have 8 numbers

$$
\begin{align}
&{\color{red}0}00 \,\,\,0 \\
&{\color{red}0}01 \,\,\,1 \\
&{\color{red}0}11 \,\,\,3 \\
&{\color{red}0}10 \,\,\,2 \\
&—— \\
&{\color{blue}1}10 \,\,\,6 \\
&{\color{blue}1}11 \,\,\,7 \\
&{\color{blue}1}01 \,\,\,5 \\
&{\color{blue}1}00 \,\,\,4 \\
\end{align}
$$

The corresponding decimal values are shown on the right.

These sequences are called Reflected Gray Codes for 1, 2 and 3 bits respectively. A Gray Code for $n$ bits is a sequence of $2^n$ different $n$-bit integers with the property that every two neighbouring integers differ in exactly one bit. A Reflected Gray Code is a Gray Code constructed in the way shown above.

---

## Input

The first line of input gives the number of cases, $N$ (at most 250000). $N$ test cases follow. Each one is a line with 2 integers: $n$ ($1 \le n \le 30$) and $k$ ($0 \le k < 2^n$).

## Output

For each test case, output the integer that appears in position $k$ of the $n$-bit Reflected Gray Code.

# 中文翻譯

我們將生成一組二進位整數序列。從以下序列開始：

$$
\begin{align}
0 \\
1 \\
— \\
\end{align}
$$

將其對水平線進行鏡射（反轉），在前半部的數字前加上 0，在後半部的數字前加上 1，你將得到：

$$
\begin{align}
{\color{red}0}0 \\
{\color{red}0}1 \\
— \\
{\color{blue}1}1 \\
{\color{blue}1}0 \\
\end{align}
$$


再次重複此步驟，你將得到 8 個數字：

$$
\begin{align}
&{\color{red}0}00 \,\,\,0 \\
&{\color{red}0}01 \,\,\,1 \\
&{\color{red}0}11 \,\,\,3 \\
&{\color{red}0}10 \,\,\,2 \\
&—— \\
&{\color{blue}1}10 \,\,\,6 \\
&{\color{blue}1}11 \,\,\,7 \\
&{\color{blue}1}01 \,\,\,5 \\
&{\color{blue}1}00 \,\,\,4 \\
\end{align}
$$


右側顯示了相對應的十進位數值。

這些序列分別被稱為 1 位元、2 位元和 3 位元的「格雷碼」（Reflected Gray Codes）。$n$ 位元的格雷碼是由 $2^n$ 個不同的 $n$ 位元整數組成的序列，其特性是每兩個相鄰的整數之間恰好只有一個位元不同。而「鏡射格雷碼」就是按照上述方式構造出來的格雷碼。

---

## Input

輸入的第一行給出測資的筆數 $N$（最多 250000 筆）。接下來有 $N$ 筆測試資料。每筆測資佔一行，包含 2 個整數：$n$（$1 \le n \le 30$）和 $k$（$0 \le k < 2^n$）。

## Output

對於每筆測資，請輸出在 $n$ 位元鏡射格雷碼中，位於位置 $k$ 的整數數值。
