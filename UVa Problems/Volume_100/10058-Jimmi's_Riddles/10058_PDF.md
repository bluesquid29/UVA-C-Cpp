# 10058 - Jimmi's Riddles

Little Jimmy is only three years old but appears to be more intelligent than even Sir Isaac Newton at the age of three. People say that Jimmy could count up to ten when he was a child of ten months. His field of interest changes with his age and at present he is researching on how to trouble his mother scientifically.

He has a book of 5000 riddles and applies them every now and then. For example, if his mother asks him "Will you stop watching Tom and Jerry?" he will say "tom hates jerry, jimmy hates tom" which means, "yes I will". Obviously his mother is in all sorts of trouble.

However, Jimmy is considerate. After all, he knows that his days will not be very smooth if he continues annoying his mother this way. So he taught her a number of ridiculous (riddle calculus) formulas so that she can quickly interpret Jimmy's riddles. But, she was a student of history and doesn't know much about ridiculous formulas.

So, you are to help her to get out of this trouble. You are to write a program that will read a number of riddles and, by using a number of ridiculous formulas, determine what they mean. The formulas are given below:

- $\text{STATEMENT} = \text{ACTION} \mid \text{STATEMENT, ACTION}$
- $\text{ACTION} = \text{ACTIVE\_LIST} \text{ VERB } \text{ACTIVE\_LIST}$
- $\text{ACTIVE\_LIST} = \text{ACTOR} \mid \text{ACTIVE\_LIST and ACTOR}$
- $\text{ACTOR} = \text{NOUN} \mid \text{ARTICLE NOUN}$
- $\text{ARTICLE} = \text{a} \mid \text{the}$
- $\text{NOUN} = \text{tom} \mid \text{jerry} \mid \text{goofy} \mid \text{mickey} \mid \text{jimmy} \mid \text{dog} \mid \text{cat} \mid \text{mouse}$
- $\text{VERB} = \text{hate} \mid \text{love} \mid \text{know} \mid \text{like}$ (or any of these followed by 's')
    

If a riddle is a valid ridiculous calculus statement, it means "YES I WILL"; otherwise it means "NO I WON'T".

---

## Input

Input file will contain a number of riddles each on a different line. Each riddle will consist of lowercase alphabetic characters and comma(s) only. The riddle may contain words not mentioned in the formulas. For each riddle, you are to determine what it means.

## Output

For each riddle the output will be 'YES I WILL' if the riddle is a valid ridiculous calculus statement and 'NO I WON'T' otherwise. The output for each riddle is to be on a separate line.

---

## Sample Input

```
the dog and a cat know goofy
jimmy kills tom
goofy hate mouse jerry
tom hates jerry , jimmy hates tom
```

## Sample Output

```
YES I WILL
NO I WON’T
NO I WON’T
YES I WILL
```

# 中文翻譯

小 Jimmy 只有三歲，但看起來比三歲時的牛頓爵士還要聰明 。人們說 Jimmy 在十個月大時就能數到十 。他的興趣隨著年齡增長而改變，目前他正在研究如何科學地煩擾他的母親 。

他有一本包含 5000 個謎語的書，並且時不時地運用它們 。例如，如果他的母親問他：「你會停止看《湯姆與傑利》嗎？」 ，他會說「tom hates jerry, jimmy hates tom」，這意味著「是的，我會」 。顯然，他的母親陷入了各種麻煩之中 。

然而，Jimmy 也是體貼的。畢竟他知道，如果繼續這樣惹惱母親，他的日子也不會太好過 。因此，他教了她一套 ridiculous（謎語微積分，riddle calculus）公式，以便她能快速解讀 Jimmy 的謎語 。但是，她曾是一名歷史系的學生，對這些 ridiculous 公式並不了解 。

因此，你的任務是幫助她擺脫困境 。你需要編寫一個程式來讀取若干謎語，並透過使用這些 ridiculous 公式來判斷它們的含義 。公式如下 ：

- $\text{STATEMENT} = \text{ACTION} \mid \text{STATEMENT, ACTION}$
- $\text{ACTION} = \text{ACTIVE\_LIST} \text{ VERB } \text{ACTIVE\_LIST}$
- $\text{ACTIVE\_LIST} = \text{ACTOR} \mid \text{ACTIVE\_LIST and ACTOR}$
- $\text{ACTOR} = \text{NOUN} \mid \text{ARTICLE NOUN}$
- $\text{ARTICLE} = \text{a} \mid \text{the}$
- $\text{NOUN} = \text{tom} \mid \text{jerry} \mid \text{goofy} \mid \text{mickey} \mid \text{jimmy} \mid \text{dog} \mid \text{cat} \mid \text{mouse}$
- $\text{VERB} = \text{hate} \mid \text{love} \mid \text{know} \mid \text{like}$ (或是上述單字後面加上 's')
    

如果一個謎語符合上述 ridiculous calculus 的陳述規則，它就代表「YES I WILL」；否則代表「NO I WON'T」 。

## 輸入

輸入檔案包含若干謎語，每一行代表一個謎語 。每個謎語僅由小寫字母和逗號組成 。謎語中可能包含公式中未提到的單字 。對於每筆測資，你必須判斷其含義 。

## 輸出

對於每筆測資，如果該謎語是有效的 ridiculous calculus 陳述，請輸出 'YES I WILL'，否則輸出 'NO I WON'T' 。每筆測資的輸出應各佔一行 。
