# 490 - Rotating Sentences

In "Rotating Sentences," you are asked to rotate a series of input sentences 90 degrees clockwise. So instead of displaying the input sentences from left to right and top to bottom, your program will display them from top to bottom and right to left.

---

## Input

As input to your program, you will be given a maximum of 100 sentences, each not exceeding 100 characters long. Legal characters include: newline, space, any punctuation characters, digits, and lower case or upper case English letters. (NOTE: Tabs are not legal characters.)

## Output

The output of the program should have the last sentence printed out vertically in the leftmost column; the first sentence of the input would subsequently end up at the rightmost column.

---

# 中文翻譯

在 "Rotating Sentences" 中，你被要求將一系列輸入的句子順時針旋轉 90 度 。因此，你的程式不再是從左到右、從上到下顯示輸入的句子，而是改為從上到下、從右到左顯示它們 。

## Input

作為程式的輸入，你最多會得到 100 個句子，每個句子的長度不超過 100 個字元 。合法的字元包括：換行符、空格、任何標點符號、數字以及英文小寫或大寫字母 。（注意：Tab 鍵不是合法字元。）

## Output

程式的輸出應將最後一個輸入的句子垂直列印在最左側的一行；而輸入的第一個句子隨後會出現在最右側的一行 。
