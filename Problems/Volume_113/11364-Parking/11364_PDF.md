# 11364 - Parking

> [!note]
> - Include the distance that Michael needs to walk back to the parking slot after shopping.
> - The order of visiting the stores can be arbitrary.

When shopping on Long Street, Michael usually parks his car at some random location, and then walks to the stores he needs. Can you help Michael choose a place to park which minimises the distance he needs to walk on his shopping round?

Long Street is a straight line, where all positions are integer. You pay for parking in a specific slot, which is an integer position on Long Street. Michael does not want to pay for more than one parking though. He is very strong, and does not mind carrying all the bags around.

---

## Input

- The first line of input gives the number of test cases, $1 \leq t \leq 100$. 
- There are two lines for each test case. 
	- The first gives the number of stores Michael wants to visit, $1 \leq n \leq 20$, 
	- and the second gives their $n$ integer positions on Long Street, $0 \leq x_i \leq 99$.

## Output

Output for each test case a line with <mark>the minimal distance Michael must walk given optimal parking</mark>.

---

## Sample Input

```
2
4
24 13 89 37
6
7 30 41 14 39 42
```

## Sample Output

```
152
70
```

# 中文翻譯

> [!note]
> - 請包含 Michael 在購物結束後走回停車位所需的距離。
> - 拜訪商店的順序可以是任意的。

當 Michael 在 Long Street 購物時，他通常會把車停在某個隨機的位置，然後步行到他需要去的商店。你能幫助 Michael 選擇一個停車位置，使他在購物時所需步行的總距離最小嗎？

Long Street 是一條直線，所有位置都是整數。你必須停在某一個指定的停車格，而該停車格的位置也是 Long Street 上的一個整數。Michael 不想支付超過一個停車位的費用。他很強壯，不介意提著所有購物袋四處走動。

---

## 輸入

- 第一行包含測資的筆數 $t$，$1 \leq t \leq 100$
- 對於每筆測資，有兩行輸入
	- 第一行給出 Michael 想拜訪的商店數量 $n$，$1 \leq n \leq 20$
	- 第二行給出這 $n$ 間商店在 Long Street 上的位置（整數），$0 \leq x_i \leq 99$

## 輸出

對於每筆測資，輸出一行，表示<mark>在最佳停車位置下，Michael 需要步行的最小總距離</mark>。
