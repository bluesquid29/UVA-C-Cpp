#Bitwise #GrayCode

# 題解

根據定義，這是一個將**二進位制(Binary)**，轉換為**格雷碼 (Gray Code)** 的函數映射。

$$
\large
x\mapsto x\oplus\lfloor x/2\rfloor
$$

比如討論 3 位元下，所有情況

$$
\large
\begin{align}
f(0) &= 0 \oplus 0 = 0 \\
f(1) &= 1 \oplus 0 = 1 \\
f(2) &= 2 \oplus 1 = 3 \quad (10_{2} \oplus 01_{2} = 11_{2}) \\
f(3) &= 3 \oplus 1 = 2 \quad (11_{2} \oplus 01_{2} = 10_{2}) \\
f(4) &= 4 \oplus 2 = 6 \quad (100_{2} \oplus 010_{2} = 110_{2}) \\
f(5) &= 5 \oplus 2 = 7 \quad (101_{2} \oplus 010_{2} = 111_{2}) \\
f(6) &= 6 \oplus 3 = 5 \quad (110_{2} \oplus 011_{2} = 101_{2}) \\
f(7) &= 7 \oplus 3 = 4 \quad (111_{2} \oplus 011_{2} = 100_{2}) \\
\end{align}
$$

在 C++ 寫法：

```
x ^ (x >> 1)
```
